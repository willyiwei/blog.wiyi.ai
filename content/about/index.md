I'm an engineering manager at Cisco, where I lead full-stack software development teams focused on intelligent wireless services and AI-driven automation.

My writing lives at the intersection of two things I do every day: building agentic AI systems in production, and leading engineers through the ambiguity of doing that at scale.

**What I write about:**

- Agentic AI architectures — what works in production, what doesn't, and why
- Engineering leadership — managing high-output teams in an AI-augmented world
- Systems thinking — how to reason about complexity before you build into it

I'm based in the Greater Seattle area. Outside of work I run (up to half marathon distance), play guitar, and follow way too many sports.
