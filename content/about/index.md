My writing covers what I spend my time thinking about: building AI systems that hold up in production, leading engineering teams through the ambiguity of doing that at scale, making deliberate decisions with money, and running.

**What I write about:**

- Engineering leadership — managing high-output teams in an AI-augmented world
- Investing — index funds, equities, and building toward financial independence
- Running — training, races, and what the sport teaches about consistency

I'm based in the Greater Seattle area. Outside of work I run (up to half marathon distance), play guitar, and follow way too many sports.
